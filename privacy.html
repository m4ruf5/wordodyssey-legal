Privacy Policy for Word Odyssey

Effective Date: May 13, 2026

Introduction

Word Odyssey is a casual word puzzle game published by MARUF. Your privacy matters to us, and we aim to keep this policy simple and easy to understand.

This Privacy Policy explains what information the app collects, how it is used, and the choices you have regarding your data.

Information We Collect

Game Progress and Settings

Word Odyssey stores your game progress locally on your device using a local database (Hive). This may include:

  • Coins and gems
  • Level progress and stars
  • Game settings and preferences

This information stays on your device and is not linked to your identity.

Crash Reports

We use Sentry to automatically detect and fix app crashes and technical issues. When a crash happens, Sentry may receive:

  • Crash logs and error details
  • Device model
  • Operating system version
  • A randomly generated anonymous device identifier (UUID)

This information helps us improve app stability and performance.

Anonymous Analytics

We use Supabase to receive anonymous gameplay analytics, such as:

  • Levels started
  • Levels completed
  • Hints used
  • General gameplay events

These analytics include the same anonymous device identifier (UUID) used for crash reporting. No personally identifying information is collected or sent.

No Personal Information Collected

Word Odyssey does not collect:

  • Names
  • Email addresses
  • Passwords
  • Phone numbers
  • Physical addresses
  • Contacts
  • Location data
  • Photos or files
  • Any other personal information

No account is required to play the game.

How We Use It

The limited information we collect is used only to:

  • Save your game progress and settings
  • Improve gameplay balance and features
  • Understand how players use the game
  • Detect and fix crashes or technical problems

We do not sell your data to anyone.

Third-Party Services

Word Odyssey uses the following third-party services:

  • Sentry - for crash reporting and app stability monitoring
  • Supabase - for anonymous analytics collection

These services process only anonymous technical and gameplay information as described above.

Data Retention

Local game progress remains on your device until the app is removed or app data is cleared.

Anonymous analytics and crash reports may be retained for a reasonable period to help improve the game and diagnose issues.

Children's Privacy

Word Odyssey is intended for users aged 13 and older.

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. Since the app does not collect personal information, there is minimal privacy risk to younger users.
